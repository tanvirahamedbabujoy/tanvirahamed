About
From a village in Mymensingh to building systems that matter.
The origin
4 life phases that made me.
2002–2013 · Phase 1
Rural Foundations & Early Entrepreneurial Sparks
Born in Dattakonia Village, Mymensingh. Sold vegetables at the local bazaar. Built weighing scales from scratch. Set up festival stalls. The entrepreneurial instinct was always there.
2014–2017 · Phase 2
Social Exploration & First Joint Ventures
Moved closer to high school. Expanded social circle. First joint ventures with friends at the Boishakhi Mela. First real lessons in partnership and retail management.
2017–2025 · Phase 3
The Urban Launchpad & Professional Genesis
Mymensingh city. University. Film & Media Studies. Self-taught digital skills. Formal careers in media, business, and volunteer leadership. This era built the 3 forces.
2025–Now · Phase 4
Executive Leadership & Venture Acceleration
Dhaka. Full-time corporate executive. Division General Secretary. Founding ProPact Studio. Leading HopeDrop pilot. Pursuing international Master's opportunities.
